Whats is Computer

Chapter 1

A computer is a machine that can store, process, and retrieve data. Computers can perform a wide range of tasks, including typing documents, sending emails, and playing games.

How computers work

  • Computers are programmed to follow instructions stored in their memory.
  • They accept data (input), process it according to rules, and produce information (output).
  • Most computers use a binary system, which uses 0 and 1 to perform tasks.
